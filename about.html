I design to live & I live to design.

I believe that creating something from nothing is wonderful. It's like tapping into the spiritual world & extracting a thing of beauty from it.

I've been teaching myself graphic design since I was 11. Although my background was in Media Studies & Literature, I've always nurtured my creative side.

I've lived in Kuwait & India for most of my youth & am now based in Toronto, where I've studied Interactive Media Design. Through my studies, I was exposed to various disciplines such as 3D Design, Motion Graphics, Web Development, VFX and so on.

I'm now focusing on a career in UI/UX design by building on my graphic design expertise & professional work experience. Above all, I'm a learner & a stranger to life, on a mission to add more cool adjectives to my portfolio.

I also like:

• Working with a creative & passionate team.

• Troubleshooting problems within constraints & finding solutions.

• A collaborative spirit with fellow creatives & clients.

• Travelling & imbibing my experiences into my art and work.

• The beginning of a project – taking undeveloped ideas & making them real.
