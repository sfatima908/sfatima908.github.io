I design to live, & I live to design.

I believe creating something from nothing is wonderful. It is like tapping into the spiritual world & extracting a thing of beauty from it.

I've been teaching myself graphic design since I was 11. Although my background was in Media Studies & Literature, I've always nurtured my creative side. I landed my first job with just a portfolio.

Having lived in Kuwait & India for most of my youth, I'm currently based in Toronto, where I've studied Interactive Media Design & graduated with a 4.0 GPA. Above all, I'm learner & a stranger to life, on a mission to add more cool adjectives to my portfolio.
