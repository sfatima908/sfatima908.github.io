UNDP Instagram

01.

Introduction

UNDP Kuwait approached me with a critical issue. Their Instagram page, which is their main touchpoint for communication about events, had a disorganised layout. There was a lot of visual pollution which they wished to rectify and streamline.

02.

Process

After conducting an in-depth research about the goals of UNDP in Kuwait and all over the world, I identified important branding patterns and utilised it to create a clean, professional and fun social media visual layout.

Official Announcements Posts
Icons for International Days
Photo Template
Quote Template
Special Event Posts
Light Version

03.

Templates

Amongst the deliverables were also templates that they could use for their in-house design team. So I also created a content guide for whoever will be taking over after my design direction. Overall, this was a fun project and I enjoyed collaborating with the client. They do important work in the country and I was happy to help.
